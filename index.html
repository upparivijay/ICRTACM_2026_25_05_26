About REVA University

REVA University is a state private university established in 2012 under the Government of Karnataka Act No. 13, located in Bengaluru, India’s leading technology hub. Recognised by the University Grants Commission (UGC) and approved by the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE), the University has earned an A+ grade from NAAC, reflecting its commitment to academic excellence and institutional quality. Its eco-friendly 43-acre campus provides a dynamic environment for learning, innovation, and holistic development.

The University offers a wide range of undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programmes, along with diploma and certificate courses across disciplines such as Engineering, Design, Commerce, Management, Law, Architecture, Liberal Studies, and Performing Arts. Each programme is designed to integrate academic rigour with industry relevance, hands-on learning, and innovation, preparing students for global careers.

At the heart of REVA’s philosophy is its guiding principle, “Educate to Enterprise,” which fosters entrepreneurial thinking, creativity, and leadership across disciplines. Students are empowered to become innovators and problem-solvers equipped to address real-world challenges.

The campus features advanced infrastructure, including smart classrooms, AR/VR labs, innovation and fabrication labs, modern libraries, sports facilities, and residential spaces. Through clubs, societies, NCC, NSS, and REVA NEST—the University’s incubation centre—students actively engage in entrepreneurship and innovation.

REVA University places a strong emphasis on research and innovation, with specialised centres in Food Science, Biotechnology, Nano Research, and Advanced Materials driving interdisciplinary exploration and impactful outcomes. The University’s Career Development Centre (CDC) plays a pivotal role in strengthening industry connections, offering students access to internships, career guidance, and excellent placement opportunities through robust corporate partnerships.

Life at REVA is vibrant, diverse, and inclusive, seamlessly blending academic excellence with cultural and extracurricular engagement. With a student-centric ecosystem, global outlook, and state-of-the-art infrastructure, REVA University ensures a well-rounded and enriching educational experience.

About the School of Applied Sciences

The School of Applied Sciences is driven by well-qualified and dedicated faculty members. It is equipped with state-of-the-art laboratories, digital classrooms, and a peaceful academic environment that supports both knowledge transfer and creation.

The school is known for:

  • Collaborations with leading industries and research organizations
  • Fostering critical thinking and problem-solving skills
  • Encouraging innovative research

The school has published numerous peer-reviewed articles in national and international journals and has also secured patents and research grants from esteemed funding bodies like DST, DBT, VGST, ICMR, and the Coir Board. Research domains span Biotechnology, Biochemistry, Chemistry, Physics, and Mathematics.

About the Department of Mathematics

The Department of Mathematics at REVA University focuses on both Pure and Applied Mathematics, catering to students from diverse academic backgrounds. It offers:

  • A full-time M.Sc. Program in Mathematics
  • Doctoral and Post-doctoral Research Programs

The department proudly celebrates the successful completion of Ph.D. degrees by 28+ research scholars. The faculty is highly qualified and actively engaged in teaching, research, and continuing education initiatives.

About the Conference

The 7th International Conference on Recent Trends in Applied and Computational Mathematics (ICRTACAM 2026) is envisioned as a premier global forum that celebrates innovation, fosters collaboration, and advances research in the field of applied and computational mathematics. The conference brings together leading academicians, researchers, scientists, and industry experts from across the world to exchange ideas, present cutting-edge research, and explore emerging trends in mathematics and its interdisciplinary applications.

Conference Highlights:

  • Keynote and invited talks by internationally renowned experts
  • Technical sessions featuring peer-reviewed research papers
  • Panel discussions on emerging research challenges
  • Presentations by young researchers and scholars

Submissions

Call for Abstract

We invite original research contributions from scholars across all fields of applied and computational mathematics.

1

Call for Research Abstract

Research abstracts within the scope of conference thematic areas, written in English (250 words), are solicited with a declaration of originality. Abstracts submitted electronically during registration will be published in the conference compendium. The abstract should explain the topic, significance, methodology, and principal findings. All abstracts are evaluated through blind peer review.

2

Call for Oral Presentations

Oral presentations are conducted in offline mode only. Presenters must be available at the venue.

Each speaker gets 15 minutes including Q&A (Microsoft PowerPoint).
Submit the final presentation by the deadline given by the organisers.
Be on-site at least 30 minutes before your session.
Carry a copy on USB. Ensure Windows OS compatibility.
3

Call for Poster Presentations

Poster presentations are in offline mode only. Submission must include: (a) 8-10-word title, (b) 25-50-word abstract, (c) 1-2-page summary, (d) 2-5 keywords. Include presenter name, address, phone, email, affiliation.
Poster Size: 90 cm (W) x 120 cm (H)

4
